WHAT DO WE DO?

Features

An Identity For Fans

The fan app acts as a loyalty card with an ID that fans can use to access unique experiences and perks directly from artists. Patent Pending.

Live Chat

Every feed post to the network has a live chat room built-in. We help fans connect in real-time with the artist and other fans.

Credit cards

Fans use their Fan.si QR ID, credit card or Apple Pay to pay for nearly anything artists want to sell. We report unit sales to Soundscan to help their charting.

Reports

All the fan data belongs to the artists. But... data without insights is useless. Our reports are filled with actionable insights to help monetize the fans. 

Custom Music

Independent and Major Label artists have varying degrees of access to their catalogs of songs. We help navigate and build the best possible music experience for fans.

Fan Support

Fans got problems? We do all the fan support so artists don't have to. 

Live Video

For select artists, we've partnered to create live experiences which extend the custom app into a live streaming tool to display feeds onto live stage video walls.

Control the devices

We can turn artist apps into part of the live show by remotely controlling the flashlights on people's phones.

VR

We've partnered with leading VR content creators to sell tickets to fans who want to see live shows via their VR headsets (including Google Cardboard).

Fanpacks

As an advanced feature, we can reward fans with exclusive gifts and sponsored/brand items, automatically shipped to fans when they sign up - and/or at predetermined intervals of their membership. The cost of the shipped item is calculated as part of the tier pricing set by the artist.
